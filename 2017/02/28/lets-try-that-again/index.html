Let's try that again!

Full on PPS attack last night. Not only did I totally bail on any plans to work out after bedtime, I also did not even attempt personal time this morning due to an untimely bathroom break for my fur baby. Willy decided to wake up the entire house at 4:30 a.m. so he could go outside. After that, I spent an hour getting my human babies back to sleep and at that point, I just cat napped for 30 minutes before I had to get up and start the day.

This morning was a bit of a fluke so we can disregard that as part of my PPS but last night was just ridiculous. I must have talked myself in and out of exercising a dozen times.

Looks like the kids will not go to bed on time tonight. I better wait and work out in the morning. No, I better work out tonight no matter how late it is because tomorrow morning the kids might be in bad moods. And what if they wake up during the night and I’m too tired? So yes, I’ll work out tonight. But, its kind of rainy outside. Where can I work out that would not include me be attacked by hundreds of gallinippers?…and on and on and on

The way to insure that my plans won’t play out as expected is to inject any amount of time, no matter how short, between conceiving plan and executing plan. That is a total plan killer. And, to no one’s surprise, my plans did not pan out.

So now, I am back at it again.

I’m going to flip it up. Tonight, after bedtime, I will actively ask myself if I would like some personal time or some exercise. This will hopefully cut back on the back and forth with what the plan is. I’m going to outsmart my PPS!
